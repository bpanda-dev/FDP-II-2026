Day 4 · Stage 4: Target prep

Characterize the Target

Now the target itself. Use a protein language model to characterize it. Annotate the domains, find the pocket, and score which mutations matter. Then prep a clean structure for docking. We stay on small molecules this week, so protein-design tools sit this one out.
