Day 1 · Stage 1: Target brief

Foundations & Target Framing

Day one is about getting your bearings. Set up the tools. Then put an LLM to work, turning a protein target into a clear, structured brief. By the end of the day you have a target, a dossier, and a first list of known actives.

Theme LLMs + prompt engineering · Tools Claude · PubMedBERT · Deliverable Stage 1 dossier + known actives
